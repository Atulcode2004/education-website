What is AI?

AI means Artificial Intelligence. It is when computers can think and learn like humans. AI can help us in many ways.

How Do We Use AI?

We use AI in phones, cars, games, and even in hospitals. It helps us do things faster and easier.

Is AI Good or Bad?

AI can be good if we use it the right way. It can help people. But we must be careful so it does not cause problems.

The Future of AI

AI will grow more in the future. It will help in schools, homes, and businesses. We must learn how to use it well.
